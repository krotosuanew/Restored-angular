Clockwise Clockware is engaged in the repair of grandfather clocks with home visits to customers.

Small watch repair

The small watch is slow, fast, stopped, stopped winding or started beeping uncharacteristic crackling / tapping? The worker will quickly determine the cause of the problem and fix it.

  • Watch diagnostics and repair
  • Repair, cleaning, lubrication, debugging of the mechanism
  • Glass replacement
  • Worker to handle in an hour
